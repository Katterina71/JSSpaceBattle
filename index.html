Space Battle: The Zorbian Siege

Welcome to the Space Battle, a text-based adventure game that unfolds within the confines of your web browser's developer console.

This game brings the thrill of intergalactic warfare to the simplicity of text commands, allowing you to command the USS Assembly in a daring conflict against a fleet of alien invaders.

Your Mission:

As the USS Assembly confronts the Zorbian fleet near a dying star, Captain Lee, amidst blaster chaos, calls for your aid in this critical battle. Your support is vital to secure a victory that will stand as a testament to human resilience across the cosmos.

Good Luck, Captain!
